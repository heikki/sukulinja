# bourbon

House of Bourbon demo dataset (303 individuals, 139 families, 47 portraits).

**Source**: [`D-Jeffrey/gedcom-samples`](https://github.com/D-Jeffrey/gedcom-samples),
`sample-bourbon/` subdirectory. Dual-licensed upstream as `MIT OR CC0-1.0`.

**Image provenance**: portraits in `bourbon/media/` originate from Wikimedia
Commons (traceable via the `original_path` column in the `media` table). They
are overwhelmingly old-master paintings of long-deceased European royalty, in
the public domain as PD-Art. If you redistribute this directory, consider
restoring per-file Wikimedia attribution, which upstream did not preserve.
